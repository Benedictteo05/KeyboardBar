Features

About

Made with stems from Halo Clears or Halo Trues, with the housing found on Invyr Panda switches, Tecsee Purple Pandas switches combine the best of two worlds. The result is a snappy, tactile switch that is satisfying to type on. This time around we won't be pre-lubing the switches. As the original tooling is too damaged to make a new run, this batch will be made with updated tooling.
